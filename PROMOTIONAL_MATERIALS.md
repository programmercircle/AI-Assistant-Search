## Common Questions & Answers (for support)

**Q: Do I need API keys?**
A: No! The extension uses your existing browser logins. Just make sure you're signed into the AI services you want to use.

**Q: Does this cost money?**
A: The extension is completely free. However, some AI services may have their own subscription plans (like ChatGPT Plus or Claude Pro).

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Absolutely! We don't collect any data. Everything stays in your browser. The extension simply opens the AI service in a new tab - exactly like clicking a bookmark.

**Q: Why isn't the text auto-filling?**
A: Make sure you're logged into the AI service. If it still doesn't work, you can manually paste the text - it's still selected for you!

**Q: Can I use this on other browsers?**
A: Currently, it's designed for Chrome and Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave, Opera). Firefox version coming soon!

**Q: How do I add/remove AI services?**
A: Click the extension icon in your toolbar to see toggles for each AI service. Turn them on or off as needed.

**Q: Does this work offline?**
A: No, you need an internet connection to access the AI services.

**Q: Can I use keyboard shortcuts?**
A: Not yet, but we're considering adding this feature in a future update!
